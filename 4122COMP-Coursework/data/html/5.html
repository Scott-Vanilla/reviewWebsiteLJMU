Professor Hershel Layton is an archaeology professor from London who, with his apprentice Luke Triton, enjoys solving puzzles. They are invited to a town known as St. Mystere by the family of the deceased Baron Augustus Reinhold, to find the golden apple which is the key to his entire fortune. However, the duo gets more than they bargained for with the town containing far more secrets than expected. To find the truth about the town exploring and puzzle solving must be done, with a possible 138 puzzles to solve this game is packed with tricky but entertaining brain teasing fun.
