Fortnite Battle Royale is underwhelming and not worth the time to play. With obvious parts taken from PUBG, it is difficult to appriciate that this game has any originality at all! The whole game relies on the loot you obtain and means that good players can be overlooked by bad luck. The game has a single limited map which grows boring after multiple instances of playing. In game purchases are pointless too and add no value to the game on the whole, and is but another money grabbing scheme. Spend your money elsewhere!
