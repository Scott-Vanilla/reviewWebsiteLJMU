The story of Kingdom hearts follows Sora, Goofy and Donald on an adventure to save King Mickey from the clutches on Ansem and the heartless. Along the way they are tasked with saving multiple disney princesses as well as defeat villans and explore mysical worlds (which we all know and love) such as: Atlantica, Halloweentown, Agrabah, Neverland etc. The overall game brings the inner child out in all who play, as your captivated by the storyline of any childs dream!
